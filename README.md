# LEO-2026: Intelligent Weightlifting Assistant

**LEO-2026** is a high-performance native Android application designed for athletes who demand precision and efficiency. Unlike generic fitness trackers, LEO-2026 acts as a "training co-pilot," adapting to your specific gym equipment and providing proactive analytics in real-time.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Proactive Plate Calculator:** An inventory-aware algorithm that calculates the exact plate combinations based on your actual bars and available weight plates. No more guessing—if you can't build a specific weight, the app suggests the closest possible alternatives.
*   **Smart Training Co-pilot:** High-intensity stopwatch system with **Auto-Start** logic. The app automatically triggers the next set's timer after your rest period ends, keeping your training tempo consistent and hands-free.
*   **Asset Management:** Fully customizable inventory for your "Training Assets" (bars, dumbbells, and plates). The app knows your physical environment.
*   **Data Integrity (Snapshot Technology):** Historical records are protected via "Data Snapshots." Renaming or deleting an exercise today won't alter your past achievements. Your history remains a faithful record of your progress.
*   **Circular UX Design:** Seamlessly launch workouts directly from your exercise library or saved routines, breaking linear navigation barriers for a fluid user experience.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Language:** Kotlin (Native Android)
*   **UI:** Jetpack Compose (Modern Declarative UI)
*   **Database:** Room Persistence Library (Local Encrypted Storage)
*   **Architecture:** MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) with Flow for reactive data streams.
